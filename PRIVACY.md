# LeLuca Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

LeLuca is a local-first meeting recording, transcription, and notes app. LeLuca
does not operate a developer-controlled server and does not include advertising,
analytics, or tracking.

## Data stored on your device

Recordings, transcripts, summaries, speaker labels, and meeting conversations
are stored in LeLuca's local app container. API keys and OAuth credentials are
stored in the system Keychain. You can delete meetings in LeLuca and remove
cloud credentials in Settings.

## Recording and transcription

LeLuca accesses the microphone only after you start a recording. If you choose
system-audio recording, macOS may request Screen Recording permission; LeLuca
uses that permission to capture audio and does not record or store screen
pixels. Speech transcription runs locally on your device. The AI notes layer
does not upload your original audio.

## Optional cloud AI services

You may use Apple Intelligence entirely on-device, or choose ChatGPT, the
OpenAI API, the Anthropic API, or OpenRouter. Each cloud provider requires your
permission before LeLuca sends transcript text.

When you request a cloud summary or ask a question, LeLuca sends only the
transcript text, instructions, and conversation context needed to complete that
request directly to the provider you selected. The provider processes that data
under its own terms and privacy policy:

- OpenAI: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/
- Anthropic: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- OpenRouter: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

LeLuca's developer does not receive your recordings, transcripts, prompts,
credentials, or provider responses.

## Update checks

The direct-download edition may make an anonymous request to GitHub Releases to
check for a newer LeLuca version. The Mac App Store edition relies on the App
Store for updates and does not perform this check.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at:
https://github.com/grep24/LeLuca-releases/issues

# LeLuca 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 10 日

LeLuca 是一款以本地处理为优先的会议录音、语音转写与会议笔记 App。LeLuca
不使用开发者自建服务器，也不包含广告、分析 SDK 或用户追踪。

## 保存在设备上的数据

录音、逐字稿、摘要、说话人标注和会议问答保存在 LeLuca 的本地 App
容器中。API Key 与 OAuth 凭据保存在系统 Keychain。您可以在 LeLuca
中删除会议，也可以在“设置”中移除云端服务凭据。

## 录音与转写

仅当您主动开始录音时，LeLuca 才会访问麦克风。如果您选择录制系统声音，
macOS 可能会请求“屏幕录制”权限；LeLuca 仅使用该权限采集音频，不会录制
或保存屏幕画面。语音转写在您的设备本地完成。AI 会议纪要层不会上传原始
音频。

## 可选的云端 AI 服务

您可以使用完全在本机运行的 Apple Intelligence，也可以选择 ChatGPT、
OpenAI API、Anthropic API 或 OpenRouter。每个云端服务都需要先取得您的
明确许可，LeLuca 才会发送逐字稿文本。

当您请求云端摘要或针对会议提问时，LeLuca 只会把完成该请求所需的逐字稿
文本、指令和对话上下文，直接发送给您选择的服务方。相关数据将依据该服务方
自己的条款与隐私政策处理：

- OpenAI：https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy/
- Anthropic：https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- OpenRouter：https://openrouter.ai/privacy

LeLuca 的开发者不会收到您的录音、逐字稿、提示词、凭据或服务方返回内容。

## 更新检查

官网直接下载版可能会向 GitHub Releases 发起匿名请求，以检查 LeLuca
新版本。Mac App Store 版由 App Store 管理更新，不会执行此项检查。

## 联系方式

如有隐私问题，请在以下地址提交 Issue：
https://github.com/grep24/LeLuca-releases/issues
